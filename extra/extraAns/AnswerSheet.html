"To protect from online data breaches every business and organisation should have.."

To protect from online threats you won't necessarily need big security guards but you should have good security training and awareness as well as risk management and incident response teams.

"the process of identifying assets, analysing, evaluating and addressing threats could describe"

Risk management is the process of identifying assets and the risks to them followed by analysing and addressing the threats.

"What is a backdoor in cyber security?"

A backdoor is a bug or an exploit used to bypass security controls and gain access to your device or network often from outdated or unpatched software.

Answer = 'A bug/exploit used to bypass security'

While Ransomware could potentially monitor your activity aswell it will make itself known and demand a ransom for locked or encrypted data.

Answer = 'Spyware'

"How would you reduce the risk of malware?"

Keeping up-to-date anti virus and malware protection, using caution when downloading and executing files and avoiding pop-ups and ads are all ways to reduce the risk of malware.

"What is a Phishing attack?"

A phishing email is generally an attack where you will receive an email attempting to trick you into giving out personal details or by getting you to sign into a false website similar to that you use regularly.

Answer = 'An attack using email or text attempting to trick you into giving personal details'

"Are viruses and worms forms of malware?"

Yes, both viruses and worms are forms of malware.

Answer = 'Yes'
